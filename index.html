I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Munich Center for Mathematical Philosophy, and I work on philosophy of science, philosophical methodologies, the history of logical empiricism, and formal methods in ethics. I received a Master's degree in physics from the Universität Hamburg and a PhD in philosophy from Utrecht University. My studies also brought me to the Universität Bayreuth, the University of Minnesota, the Universität Augsburg, the University of Western Ontario, and Tilburg University.
